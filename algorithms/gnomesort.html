Gnome Sort

Gnome Sort (stupid sort) is based on the concept of a garden gnome sorting his flower pots.
A garden gnome sorts the flower pots by the following method-


He looks at the flower pot next to him and the previous one.
If they are in the right order, he takes a step one pot forward, if, he swaps them and takes a step one pot backwards.
If he is at the beginning of the pot line, he steps forwards. If he is at the end of the pot line, he is done.


1. If you are at the beginning of the array, go to the element to the right (from arr[0] to arr[1]).
2. If the current element is larger or equal to the previous array element, take a step right.
3. If the current element is smaller than the previous array element then swap these two elements and go one step backwards.
4. Repeat steps 2. and 3. until the index (i) reaches the end of the array (-1).
5. If it has reached the end of the array stop moving.
